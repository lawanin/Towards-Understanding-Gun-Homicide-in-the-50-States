This changelog describes key changes to the dataset that accompany each release. It particularly focuses on changes that impact estimates for prior years. For full details see methodology of accompanying report. 

March 1st, 2021 Release: Added data for 2017. Rates for the years 2010-2016 have been recalculated with vintage 19 population data. Estimates of abortion by state of residence for the year 2015 are slightly different than published in October 2nd data release, as the Stineman interpolation formula leverages data from an additional year.
